Jarangakanutyun/Inheritance
	Jarangakanutyuny hnaravorutyun e talis class-in 
	janargel parametrer ev method-ner ir parent class-ic

Encapsuation
	Encapsulacian mexanizm e vory sahmanapakum e mutqy classi 
	methodnerin ev parametrerin,dranq darnum en hasaneli miayn 
	classi nersum,katarvum e private ev protected modifikatornerov

Polymorphism
	Polymorphism-y mi shablon e vorum class-ner karox en unenal 
	tarber functionalner,bayc ogtagorcven mek yndhanur interface-ov

Abstruct
	Abstructy ayn class-n e vori object-ner chenq karox stexcel,dranq 
	stecvum en miayn ir jarangneric, isk ete method-y abstruct e apa 
	nra irakanacumy(marminy/body-n) bacakayum e,irakanacumy jarangnerum petq e partadir katarvi

Interface
	Inteface gorciq e vory cuyc e talis te inch method petq e 
	parunaki class-y aranc ayd method-i nkaragrutyan

Trait
	Trait-nery xmbavorum en voroshaki functional, vorpesszi class-um drancic ogtvenq
